This story begins November 2012: at a dive bar in South St. Louis

(My son's 4th Birthday party.)

My crazy aunt brought a gift that was a surprise to both of us.

Her name is Catwoman, but we call her Kitty.

She wants you to believe she is the perfect pet.

  • She always comes when she's called.
  • Referees the Nerf games.
  • Assists in walking the dog.
  • Holds the remotes so we can always find them.
  • Does math homework without being told.

This is the part where I let the cat out of the bag.

Or let the cat let herself out of the window.

This kitty is a Cold, Calculating, Calico cat.

While she appears to be enjoying a casual evening stroll with the fam, she is actually taking advantge of the perfect opportunity to seek out potential victims.
